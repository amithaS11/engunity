1. Practice Deep Breathing

Take a few minutes to focus on your breath. Inhale deeply through your nose, hold for a few seconds, and exhale slowly.

2. Take a Walk

Step outside for some fresh air and a change of scenery. A short walk can help clear your mind.

3. Listen to Music

Play your favorite songs or calming tunes to lift your spirits and relax your mind.

4. Try Meditation

Spend a few minutes meditating to improve focus and reduce stress.

5. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to stay refreshed and energized throughout the day.

6. Get Enough Sleep

Prioritize rest to manage stress effectively

7.Unplug

Disconnect from technology to recharge mentally

8.Stretch Your Body

Release tension with simple stretches or yoga poses

9.Set Boundaries

Learn to say no to reduce overwhelming commitments
